Service

How I have given back

In High School I have volunteered many times at the Austin Hindu Temple. This was an amazing oppurtunity to help the local people set up some of the biggest hindu celebrations, and serve food to less fortunate people. I have volunteered for them for two years prior to moving to college.

In high school and college, I volunteered Johnson's Backyard Farms, a local farm in Austin, TX that grows organic produce. Me and a group of 3 went to the farm and helped bag local produce, which is 100% volunteer driven.

In High school I volunteered many times for the viper tennis team that I was apart of. For two years we held a clinic for kids in the community, teaching young kids the basics of tennis. Another event I volunteered for was a community doubles tournament that was also sponsored by our team. I helped set up and manage a local doubles tournaments, played by members of the community. I volunteered for a total of 4 years for Viper Tennis!

My plans for the future

Because of this, I know it is only right to give back what I have been so fortunate to receive throughout my life.
